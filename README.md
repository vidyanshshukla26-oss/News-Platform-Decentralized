🌐 Overview

<img width="1918" height="1047" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/130c1937-9efc-45af-9211-d9d9da308fd4" />


Traditional news platforms are centralized — meaning:

Content can be censored or manipulated

Algorithms are opaque

Users have zero ownership

This project introduces a decentralized alternative, inspired by systems similar to distributed networks where no single authority controls the flow of information.

Decentralized platforms aim to provide censorship resistance, transparency, and user empowerment

✨ Key Features

🔹 Decentralized Architecture

No central authority controlling content

Distributed storage & validation

🔹 User-Owned Content

Publish news freely

Cryptographic identity (future scope)

🔹 Censorship Resistance

Content cannot be easily removed or altered

🔹 Trust & Verification Layer

Community-driven validation system

Reputation-based credibility (planned)

🔹 Real-Time News Sharing

Fast and open dissemination of information

🔹 Open Source & Transparent

Fully auditable codebase

Community-driven improvements

🧠 Why Decentralization?

Centralized systems often suffer from:

Bias in content delivery

Data exploitation

Lack of transparency

Decentralized systems solve this by:

Eliminating single points of failure

Giving users control over identity and data

Ensuring tamper-proof publishing
